Rules

Each player will submit 10 questions and answers. Each player will take their turn as the Quiz Master. As the Quiz Master, each player will read their questions and the other players will submit their answers for the given Quiz Master. Before submitting their answers, players may revisit previous questions from the round and edit their answers.

When all players have submitted their answers, the Quiz Master will grade each answer. One point will be awarded for each correct answer. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins!
